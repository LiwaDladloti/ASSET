Funding Information

ASSET relies on donor funding to continue its contribution to education in the Western Cape. The growth in the number of young people we have been able to support over the years is due to the generosity of our donors and of the trusts and funds we administer and we express our deep gratitude for the part they are playing in our work. The need however is very great and we wish to strengthen and expand our current work. We can provide a great partnership with individuals or foundations who wish to make a real difference to the education in South Africa that will change the lives of the youth for the better.
